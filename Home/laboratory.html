Project Objectives 
        Modular Risc-V laptop that would allow for every component to be swapped out, upgraded
        and repaired easily. Cost effective solution for educational or professional environments,
        Synthix Labs aims to have a Open Source Laptop that would contribute to helping technology
        advance forward,
